# EagleEye — Full-Stack Ecommerce Automation Platform

EagleEye is an AI-powered platform that automates the entire lifecycle of launching and running an ecommerce business — from the first spark of an idea all the way to driving sales.

---

## What It Does

### 1. Idea Generation
- Analyzes market trends, competitor gaps, and demand signals to surface winning product niches
- Scores and ranks ideas by profitability potential, competition level, and barrier to entry
- Generates a full business concept including brand name, positioning, and target audience

### 2. Product Sourcing
- Automatically identifies and vets suppliers (AliExpress, Alibaba, domestic wholesalers)
- Compares pricing, MOQs, shipping times, and supplier reliability scores
- Generates outreach templates and negotiation scripts for supplier communication

### 3. Store Building
- Scaffolds a complete ecommerce storefront (Shopify / custom stack) from a single prompt
- Auto-generates product listings with SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, and images
- Sets up payment processing, shipping rules, and tax configuration

### 4. Marketing Automation
- Creates a full go-to-market plan: content calendar, ad copy, email flows, and social strategy
- Launches and manages paid ad campaigns (Meta, TikTok, Google) with AI-optimized targeting
- Monitors performance and reallocates budget automatically based on ROAS

---

## Vision

Most people have ecommerce ideas but lack the time, capital, or expertise to execute. EagleEye collapses the gap between idea and revenue — what used to take a team of specialists months to build, EagleEye delivers end-to-end in hours.

## Core Modules

| Module | Responsibility |

| `IdeaEngine` | Market research, niche scoring, concept generation |
